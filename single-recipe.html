scrambled with crème fraîche

By pryary, nothing is simpler than scrambled eggs... and yet, it can happen that they end up overcooked and a little dry. With this unmissable recipe for scrambled eggs with crème fraîche, gourmet breakfasts and brunches are yours. Accompany them, as you wish, with lumpfish roe, strips of smoked salmon, mushrooms or diced ham. And to give them a festive look, place them on blinis with a hint of caviar!

prep time

30 min.

cook time

5 min.

serving

1 serving

Tags : beef breakfast pancakes food

instructions

step 1

Place the eggs in a bowl, add salt and pepper to taste.

step 2

Using a fork, beat them lightly.

step 3

Add the crème fraîche and beat more vigorously.

step 4

Melt the butter in a slightly high frying pan, making sure to grease the entire surface and walls well.

step 5

for the eggs into the pan over low heat. Mix continuously using a wooden spoon until coagulated. The eggs should remain soft and creamy.

step 6

Remove the pan from the heat to stop cooking and serve immediately. Add salt and pepper.

ingredients

1 C. soup sour cream

2 eggs

15 g Butter

Mill pepper

tools

Hand Blender

Large Heavy Pot With Lid
